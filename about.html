About Us

Absolute Technology Consulting Solutions Started solely as a structured cabling company by two brothers! “Our Father taught us to work Smarter not Harder” Says – One Brother; While the other one will speak of craft and being proud of your work. Over the Past 10 years AbTechCs.com has expanded and grown to offer more services that include all types of cabling, Wi-Fi and Site Surveys, Fire alarms, CCTV & IP Camera Systems, access control , audio/video solutions, security Systems, Paging/intercom , Fiber optics, testing, inspection, and system monitoring. AbTech is also now partnered with some of the biggest names in the industry coupled with our 24×7 staff.

ABTECHS is pivoted on serving its customers with excellence.

We have the resources to cover any rollout or project need. Abtech has earned the distinction for high quality, honest construction with a profound appreciation for authentic design and superior craftsmanship. AbTechCs.com works intimately with clients and team members establishing a unique design-build relationship that ensures every project is held to the exacting standards of design and spirit of place. AbTechCs.com’s philosophy has remained grounded in the simple concept that architecture and sound craftsmanship should be seamlessly and exquisitely united. Building exceptionall designed and crafted projects is an endeavor based on the unique relationship between you, the project manager, and our full support staff.

These partnerships are just as important as any design or construction material. Instilling trust and effective communication is essential to building lasting relationships, and this mutual respect provides peace of mind throughout the entire building process. By combining valued relationships with a solid focus on superior craftsmanship and honest design, Abtech’s dedication to your project results in something truly beautiful.

With our years of experience helping some of the nation’s largest companies execute highly-efficient, error-free deployments in the areas of network infrastructure, AV, and security.
